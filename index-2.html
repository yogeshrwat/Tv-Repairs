LCD TV Repair

If your Liquid-Crystal Display television (LCD TV) suffers from any of the following call us. Stuttering or Flickering, Vertical Lines, Dead Pixels, Buzzing, Incorrect Resolution or Random Shutoffs.

LED TV Repair

If your Light Emitting Diodes television (LED TV) suffers from any of the following call us. Backlight Issues, Double Images, Stuck Pixels, Buzzing, Blurred Images or Not Turning On.

Plasma TV Repair

If your Plasma television suffers from any of the following call us today. Screen Reflection, No Sound, Screen Burn, Phosphor Trails, Turns Off On It's Own or Doesn't Turn On.

DLP TV Repair

If your Digital Light Processing television or projector (DLP TV) suffers from any of the following call us. Bad Bulb, No Power, No Picture, No Sound, Flashing Red or Dots On The Screen.
